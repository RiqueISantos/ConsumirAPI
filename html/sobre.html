Sobre La Mejor Pizzaria

Founded in 1958 in the USA, La Mejor Pizzaria is the largest pizza chain in the world, with a presence in 100 countries and more than 16,000 restaurants across five continents.

The brand employs over 250,000 people globally. Every day, more than 4 million customers are served at La Mejor Pizzaria locations around the world.

Present in Brazil since 1989, La Mejor Pizzaria currently has more than 230 restaurants spread across 24 states and over 60 cities.

In the country, La Mejor Pizzaria is part of IMC – International Meal Company – a publicly traded company that also owns the brands KFC, Frango Assado, Viena, Olive Garden, among others.
